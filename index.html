About Us

Who Are We?

A Saudi brand. We have long experience in serving ice cream in the traditional Italian way in carts. It is available to individuals and companies on occasions, weddings and various events. There are also sizes and types of carts presented to you according to... For the appropriate type. The vehicle is delivered to the desired location and can be chosen Flavors upon request.
